Introduction

Hi there! I'm Francis from Singapore and welcome to my movie fan-page. As you can tell from the picture above, I'm a fan of the Lord of the Rings (LOTR) trilogy. Even though the films were good, I personally liked the books better as the world that Tolkien built was much more richer and flavoured than was shown in the films. For example, the important (but brief) appearance of Tom Bombadil in the first book was something many fans of the books missed in the movies. Now this is not an ad for the books, but if you liked the movies then you really consider reading the books.
